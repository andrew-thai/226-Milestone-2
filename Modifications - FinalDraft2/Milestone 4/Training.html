About this course:

Course in Indigenous Mentoring comprises a five day classroom component and practicum involving a community or workplace mentoring project.

Course skill outcomes:

Mentoring Indigenous workers for job retention, Work with diverse people, Use communication to build relationships

HLT30113 Certificate III in Aboriginal and/or Torres Strait Islander Primary Health Care

Prerequisites:

Applications for this course are open only to people who identify as being Aboriginal and/or Torres Strait Islander descent.

How is the course delivered:

The Certificate III in Aboriginal and/or Torres Strait Islander Primary Health Care may be delivered in a flexible work-based mode combining on and off-the-job activities for delivery and assessment. Off-the-job delivery and on-the-job delivery is negotiated with the participant and their workplace. Clinical skills are developed and practiced in a simulated environment as well as in the workplace clinic. Participants need to be employed in a role utilising clinical skills or with direct client interaction and care, practical placements or job rotations will be required.

HLT20113 Certificate II in Aboriginal and/or Torres Strait Islander Primary Health Care

Prerequisites:

Applications for this course are open only to people who identify as being of Aboriginal and/or Torres Strait Islander descent who are employed in a health care environment.

How is the course delivered:

The Certificate II in Aboriginal and/or Torres Strait Islander Primary Health Care is delivered flexibly. This is a foundation course for those employed in a health care environment and will provide participants with skills and knowledge required to perform their jobs. Some skills will be practiced and developed in a simulated environment and some will be assessed on-the-job.
